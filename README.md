# CMT Phase 2 — Interactive Demo

Prototype for Shopee's Campaign Management Tool (CMT) Phase 2, exploring an AI agent layer for campaign production.

## Try it

Open the demo: **[index.html](./index.html)** (live at the GitHub Pages URL once enabled)

## Demo flow

The prototype walks through four stages of a campaign Playbook:

1. **Briefing** — start from a Business Requirement
2. **Planning** — AI proposes decisions (LP Building, Deal Hunting, Creative Workstream, In-App Asset Plotting, Voucher Workstream)
3. **Production** — approved decisions spawn artifacts
4. **Execution** — artifacts dispatched to executors

Try two paths:

- **Manual co-authoring** — click "Proceed to next step →" to walk through each stage, or click decisions and approve them individually to spawn artifacts incrementally.
- **Re-run from precedent** — on the Briefing stage, click "Re-run from past campaign →" to watch a ~10-second scripted replay that demonstrates the leverage argument: subsequent campaigns auto-replay from past playbook structure.
